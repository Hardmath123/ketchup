Ketchup

Scenario: You discover a webcomic and instantly fall in love with it. But the webcomic has been around for four years now… how can you possibly catch up?

Enter Ketchup! Ketchup is a temporal RSS proxy. It transforms RSS feeds to deliver articles to you at your preferred pace, starting from the very beginning.
